Sunbeam Wide Slot 4-Slice Toaster, Black

Make toast just the way you like with the Sunbeam Wide Slot 4-Slice Toaster. This sleek black

American Electrical Standards, the Sunbeam Wide Slot 4-Slice Toaster is backed by a one-year limited warranty.
